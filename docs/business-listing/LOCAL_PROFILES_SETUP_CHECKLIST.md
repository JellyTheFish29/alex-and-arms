# Local Profiles Setup Checklist (Alex & Arms Ceramica)

Use this to set up and optimize local visibility across Google, Bing, and Apple.

## 0) Prep once (before any platform)

- Confirm official business name (exact spelling).
- Confirm address format (single canonical version).
- Confirm primary phone number.
- Confirm primary website URL.
- Confirm business category (primary + secondary).
- Prepare business description (short + long versions).
- Collect logo, cover photo, showroom photos, team photos.
- Prepare business hours, holiday hours, and parking info.

## 1) Google Business Profile (Highest priority)

- Claim or request access.
- Set primary category and relevant secondary categories.
- Add exact NAP:
  - Name, Address, Phone.
- Add website link.
- Add business description (natural, no keyword stuffing).
- Add products/services where relevant.
- Upload strong photos (exterior, interior, products, team).
- Add attributes:
  - Parking availability, payment types, etc.
- Verify hours and special hours.
- Enable messaging/call options if the team can respond quickly.
- Start review workflow:
  - Ask real customers.
  - Respond to all reviews.

## 2) Bing Places for Business

- Create/claim Bing Places listing.
- Import from Google profile if available (faster), then verify details.
- Ensure category and NAP exactly match Google.
- Add website, hours, photos, and description.
- Complete verification.

## 3) Apple Business Connect

- Create/claim listing in Apple Business Connect.
- Verify NAP consistency with Google/Bing.
- Add business category, website, hours, and photos.
- Confirm map pin location is accurate.
- Add parking and showroom-relevant details.

## 4) Core consistency checks (all platforms)

- Business name is exactly the same everywhere.
- Address format is exactly the same everywhere.
- Phone number is exactly the same everywhere.
- Website URL is exactly the same everywhere.
- Hours are identical everywhere.
- Category intent is aligned everywhere.

## 5) Citation baseline (after main profiles)

- Submit to a small set of trusted business directories first.
- Keep NAP identical on every listing.
- Track each listing in a sheet:
  - Platform, URL, login owner, status, last updated date.

## 6) Ongoing monthly maintenance

- Check for duplicate listings and remove/merge.
- Add new photos monthly.
- Verify hours and contact details.
- Monitor and respond to new reviews.
- Review profile insights (calls, direction requests, search terms).
- Update seasonal/service messaging when needed.

## 7) Success metrics to track

- Branded and local impressions.
- Calls from profile.
- Direction requests.
- Website clicks from profiles.
- Review count and rating trend.

## Quick execution order

1. Google Business Profile
2. Bing Places
3. Apple Business Connect
4. Citation cleanup and consistency tracking
5. Monthly maintenance routine
